My Roots

Growing up, I watched my mother run a shop where her prices were always lower than neighbouring stores. When I asked her why, she said: 'The more we sell to the poor, the more reasonable the price we set. It's called sharing.' I was a child, but her response planted something that has stayed with me since: working hard, keeping my integrity, and supporting the community.

It's why getting close to customers, and understanding what the numbers say about them, is how I seek to serve people better. Every step of the way has been an intentional choice to work in the industry of people.
